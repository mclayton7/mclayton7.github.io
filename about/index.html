Born and raised in Chicago, I grew up with a love for creating things.

Before designing, I studied film at Columbia College and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts. I was a video editor for many years before discovering 8th Light.

I studied design under Stephanie Briones, where I learned to keep things simple. I believe that good design should not only be beautiful but also easy to use.

When I'm not designing you will find me running, playing video games, and going out to dinner with my friends.
